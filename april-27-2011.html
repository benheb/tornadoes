Deadly Tornado Outbreak - April 25-28, 2011

April 25-28, 2011 the largest tornado outbreak ever recorded. In all, the NWS confirmed 358 tornadoes, with 205 touching down on April 27. Tragically, 348 people were killed as a result of the outbreak.
